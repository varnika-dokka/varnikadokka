## Introduction
This site is where I share my work in product management, software engineering, and AI product development. It covers the projects I've built, the teams I've worked with, and a bit about who I am outside of work.

## Pages
- **Home** - Introduction, guiding values, and links to connect
- **Projects** - Detailed breakdowns of Cache, NeuroLearn, the UW Housing & Climate Risk Dashboard, and LearnMate
- **Experience** - Professional roles and product & research work
- **Background** - Education, leadership, and activities
- **About** - Why PM, a little about me, my toolkit, and life outside work
